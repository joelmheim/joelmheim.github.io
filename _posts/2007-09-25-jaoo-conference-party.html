--- layout: post section-type: post title: JAOO Conference party date: '2007-09-25T09:07:00.000+02:00' author: Jørn Ølmheim category: tech tags: [ 'jaoo', 'jruby' ] modified_time: '2007-09-25T09:24:25.776+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-16320608.post-270170299132768401 blogger_orig_url: http://joelmheim.blogspot.com/2007/09/jaoo-conference-party.html --- Monday night is really the highlight of the JAOO conference for me. I talked about this last year as well, and the first day of JAOO I allways feel a little strange. Then after the Monday night party the mood of the conference totally changes. I was lucky enough to end up on next to Thomas Enebo and Charles Nutter (JRuby). And as if that wasn't enough. Martin Fowler had decided that he would like to meet them too, and ended up between me and Charles. On my other side I had Jonas Bonér (Terracotta) and across the table was Floyd Marinescu (InfoQ, formerly TheServerSide). As you can imagine it was not boring... I actually managed to talk some sense and actually got a smile or two (and something I like to interpret as a nod of approval) from Martin Fowler. I talked a lot with Floyd as well, and got a chance to tell him that I enjoy his site. Charles and Thomas are really interesting guys. They are the lead developers of JRuby, the Ruby implementation on the JVM. In order to do this you have to know both the Ruby language and the JVM really well. I particularly talked a lot with Thomas, and found out that he actually has ancestry in Norway. A great evening.
